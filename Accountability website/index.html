Accountability

Accountability is the quality of a person to own up to their actions and take responsibility for their mistakes. If a team member is lacking accountability, they will lose the trust of their fellow team members and potentially hurt the team more than if they were accountable.

Accountability is the glue that holds the teams efforts together!

How to Implement Accountability

One could implement a sort of honor code into the team to increase Accountability. The team could punish people who own up to their mistakes much less than someone who doesn't, or not punish them at all! There could also be incentives for being accountable, like bonuses or raises.

How Accountability Helps Business

Accountability is the base of trust in business. If a person cannot be held accountable for their own actions, then that person is not trustworthy. By ensuring that your team and employees are held accountable, you can foster trust among the team as well as among potential business partners.
